Creativity and Technology Sparks Wonder!

I'm currently studying Human Media Technology and creating interactive prototypes as a master's student at Waseda University. I've studied how to make dandelion puffs move in a life-like manner using static electricity and I'm building a conversation facilitating robot that works for any language.

For my dandelion project, I received 2nd Prize at ISCA 2019 – an international creative award, and presented at Entertainment Computing 2020.

I contributed to a mental health web application project at JUNCTION TOKYO 2019 – a non-profit hackathon by creating a dynamic animating backgrond that reflects the user's mood.
